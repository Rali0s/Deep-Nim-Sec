Strategy: Human Risk & Counter-SE Platform (UI and Profit Model)
The platform's core thesis is: To build effective defense (NIST Controls), we must master the attacker's perspective (Grey Hat SE Manuals).

I. Reframing the Threat Model (Integrating the Manuals)
The "SE Manuals" are now the platform's proprietary Human Threat Intelligence Library.

Offensive Source (Manuals)	Defensive Mapping (NIST Controls)	Educational Flow
Pretexting Scripts, Vishing Lures	AT-2 (Security Awareness), PL-4 (Rules of Behavior)	Simulating the exact attack vector to measure employee reaction time and adherence to policy.
Physical Access Tactics (Tailgating)	PE-2 (Physical Access Auth), PS-7 (External Personnel Security)	Training employees to recognize and challenge unauthorized access based on context.
Digital Lures (Phishing Lures)	SI-4 (System Monitoring/Email Filters), IR-2 (Incident Response Training)	Training employees on incident reporting and the technical staff on TTP detection.

Summary of Profit Strategy
By starting with high-value, realistic threat content (the SE manuals), we build a platform that serves three distinct, profitable markets:

Training Market: Selling high-quality, continuous training and policy reinforcement content.
Product Market: Licensing the software platform (UI) for management, tracking, and metric generation.
Service Market: Offering premium consulting, certification, and hands-on lab environments for technical specialists and executive strategy.
